Event Schedule for Coming Months

April 27 - April 31

Desolation Canyon

Orientation starts at 7am on Tuesday. We will cast off about 8am. We will return between noon and 4:30pm on Friday. Food and equipment are provided. For clothing suggestions, check out our tips section. The weather is expected to be warm during the day, but chilly at night.

This is a beginner trip with occasional class II rapids. Some hiking is required as we camp for the night and explore the local area. Ages 7 and up is the recommended age.

May 7

Clear Creek

We are meeting at our parking lot, on Wednesday, the 7th at 6am to drive up to Idaho Springs. We will leave about 6:30am. Don't be late. It is a 5 1/2 hour trip, and we will stop for lunch. We will return around 10pm. Food and equipment are provided. Wear light clothing.

Recommended minimum age is 14. This is an class IV rapid, and previous experience and strong paddling skills are a necessity. The rafting trip will take about 3 and a half hours.

May 16

Payette River

We will meet at the Boise Idaho Airport at 10:30am on Tue. May 17th. You will have to schedule and buy your own airline tickets. From there we will drive up to the boat launch, about an hour away.

This is a class V rapid, expert only, ages 18 and up.
