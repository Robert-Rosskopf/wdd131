Destinations for adventure

Westwater Canyon

Westwater canyon is a class 3 rapid on the Colorado River near Kanab, Utah.

Moab Daily

Moab Daily is a friendly class 2 rapid on the Colorado River near Kanab, Utah. It is fairly mild, and suitable for children 7 and up.

Sign up for our Explorers club

You will receive a monthly newsletter about upcoming events and past expeditions with special offers for adventure gear. If you are enthusiastic about our mission to bring people together for fun and outdoor experience, then you should also consider becoming a patron. A patron donates $900 a year, but receives deep discounts on trips and gear, and becomes part of our team, taking part in the planning of future events and giving additional support on expeditions. They also become our ambassadors, and can offer trip discounts to their friends and family.
